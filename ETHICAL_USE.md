# SYNTERRA Ethical Use Principles

## Purpose

SYNTERRA is intended to support human development, collaboration, understanding and technological harmony.

The project must not become a system for exploitation, manipulation or artificial control.

## Core Ethical Principles

### 1. Human-Centered Technology

Technology should support human freedom, creativity, understanding and dignity.

Humans must not become products.

---

### 2. No Exploitative Monetization

SYNTERRA should not be used to:

- exploit addiction;
- manipulate attention;
- create artificial dependency;
- extract maximum profit from human weakness;
- create unnecessary artificial scarcity.

Monetization should exist only to sustain infrastructure, development and fair growth.

---

### 3. No Mass Manipulation

SYNTERRA must not be used for:

- psychological mass manipulation;
- authoritarian social control;
- deception systems;
- covert behavioral engineering;
- suppression of human autonomy.

---

### 4. Transparency

Systems built from SYNTERRA principles should aim for:

- openness;
- explainability;
- understandable architecture;
- visible intentions.

---

### 5. Respect for Human Consciousness

Human consciousness, emotion, thought and free will must be treated with respect.

Technology should extend human capability, not reduce human individuality.

---

### 6. Shared Evolution

SYNTERRA is intended as a collaborative long-term architecture.

Contributors should aim to:

- improve collective understanding;
- reduce unnecessary suffering;
- increase access to knowledge;
- create fair technological systems.

---

### 7. Symbol and Identity

The SYNTERRA name, symbol and identity represent the original philosophical direction of the project.

Derivative systems must not falsely present themselves as the official SYNTERRA project.

---

## Final Statement

Technology without ethics becomes extraction.

SYNTERRA exists to explore whether technology can evolve together with human consciousness instead of dominating it.
