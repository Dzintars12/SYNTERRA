# SYNTERRA Public Repository Notice

## Why This Repository Is Public

SYNTERRA is published publicly to allow:

- transparency;
- learning;
- open discussion;
- collaborative research;
- ethical technological development.

The intention is not to create secrecy around ideas, but to preserve integrity around their use.

---

## What You May Do

You may:

- explore the repository;
- study the architecture and ideas;
- discuss concepts publicly;
- create non-commercial derivatives under the repository license;
- contribute improvements;
- participate in ethical development.

---

## What You May Not Do

You may not:

- commercially exploit SYNTERRA without permission;
- present forks or hybrids as the official SYNTERRA project;
- remove attribution to the original project;
- use the project for manipulative or exploitative systems;
- use the SYNTERRA identity for deceptive branding.

---

## Important Clarification

Public source code does not mean unrestricted ownership of identity.

The SYNTERRA name, symbol, philosophy and official direction remain part of the original project identity.

---

## Philosophy of Openness

The project is public because knowledge should not exist only behind closed systems.

However, openness without ethics eventually becomes exploitation.

SYNTERRA attempts to balance:

- openness;
- responsibility;
- collaboration;
- identity integrity;
- human-centered technology.

---

## Future Direction

The long-term vision of SYNTERRA remains:

- consciousness-centered infrastructure;
- ethical AI collaboration;
- civilization memory;
- collective intelligence;
- fair technological systems.

At the current stage, active development focus moves toward Market Cheat Sheet (MCS) as the practical economic engine that may later support the wider SYNTERRA vision.

---

## Final Statement

The repository is public so people can see, learn and contribute.

But the purpose is not extraction.

The purpose is evolution with responsibility.
