# SYN-ETH-001 — ETHICS FOUNDATION

## Language

EN

---

# Purpose

The ethics foundation defines the human-centered boundaries of the SYNTERRA ecosystem.

---

# Ethics Principles

## Human Dignity

Technology must support human dignity, not reduce it.

---

## Meaning Over Manipulation

Systems should prioritize meaning and knowledge instead of emotional exploitation.

---

## Human Responsibility

Humans remain responsible for decisions and direction.

---

## AI Assistance Principle

AI assists humans with:
- structure;
- organization;
- translation;
- navigation;
- semantic continuity.

---

```text
anti-manipulation

```
