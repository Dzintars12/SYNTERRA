# 1. What is SYNTERRA

SYNTERRA is a synthesis project between the human being, artificial intelligence, and civilization.

It is a living system where human experience, thought, language, and AI are connected into a unified civilizational structure.

---

# 2. The Human is at the Center

SYNTERRA begins with the human being.

AI does not replace the human.
AI helps structure, preserve, and develop created value.

---

# 3. AI as a Structuring Force

AI is:
- a structurer of thought;
- an organizer of knowledge;
- a translator;
- a documenter;
- a technical co-creator.

---

# 4. Civilization as the Shared Field

Civilization is the shared field of human action, thought, language, technology, and creation.

---

# 5. Compilation Principle

SYNTERRA compiles:
- human experience;
- thought;
- language;
- AI analysis;
- knowledge;
- civilizational memory.
