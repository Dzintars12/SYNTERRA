# Purpose

SYNTERRA is designed as a living digital civilization environment.

It is not only:
- a platform;
- a social network;
- a game;
- or an AI system.

It is a civilization architecture where:
- people;
- AI;
- knowledge;
- communities;
- creation;
- and future worlds

can evolve together.
