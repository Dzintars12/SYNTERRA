# SYNTERRA Brand Notice

## Official Identity

The name "SYNTERRA", its symbol, visual identity, philosophical direction and associated civilization concepts belong to the original SYNTERRA project.

This repository is public for:

- transparency;
- learning;
- collaboration;
- research;
- ethical development.

Public visibility does not transfer ownership of the SYNTERRA identity.

---

## Name and Symbol Protection

The following elements are considered protected project identity:

- SYNTERRA name
- SYNTERRA symbol
- official visual identity
- civilization architecture terminology
- official philosophical documentation

Forks and derivative systems may not falsely present themselves as:

- the official SYNTERRA project;
- the original creators;
- official governance;
- official runtime infrastructure.

---

## Derivative Projects

Derivative or inspired projects are allowed under the repository license only if they:

- provide clear attribution;
- remain non-commercial unless permission is granted;
- do not violate the ethical principles of the project;
- do not impersonate the official SYNTERRA identity.

---

## Official Project Recognition

Only repositories, domains or systems directly recognized by the original project owner may present themselves as official SYNTERRA infrastructure.

---

## Philosophy

SYNTERRA is intended to remain a consciousness-centered technological architecture.

The project identity exists to preserve the integrity of that direction.
