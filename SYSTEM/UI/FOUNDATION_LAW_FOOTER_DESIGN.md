# SYNTERRA FOUNDATION LAW FOOTER DESIGN

## Purpose

The FOUNDATION LAW must be present across SYNTERRA as an aesthetic, calm, permanent ethical anchor.

It must not look like:

- a legal warning;
- a corporate terms notice;
- a government document;
- an aggressive compliance banner;
- a noisy footer element.

It must feel like:

- the system’s foundation;
- a digital stone;
- a silent ethical anchor;
- the core law on which SYNTERRA stands.

---

## Global Footer Principle

Every SYNTERRA page, interface, AI agent view, runtime environment and public-facing module must include a visible reference to the FOUNDATION LAW.

The footer must be minimal, elegant and always accessible.

The full document should not be displayed at the bottom of every page.

Instead, each footer must contain:

1. a short active status line;
2. one core sentence;
3. a link to the full Foundation Law page.

---

## Mandatory Footer Text

```text
SYNTERRA FOUNDATION LAW ACTIVE
Human consciousness, dignity and freedom remain above any algorithm, infrastructure or artificial intelligence.
View Foundation Law
```

---

## Link Target

The footer link must point to the English reference version:

```text
/FOUNDATION_LAW_EN.md
```

In web runtime environments, this may be rendered as a designed page:

```text
/foundation-law
```

The designed page must preserve the same legal and semantic content as `FOUNDATION_LAW_EN.md`.

---

## Visual Style

Recommended aesthetic direction:

- dark or very calm background;
- soft contrast;
- minimal typography;
- generous spacing;
- no visual noise;
- no aggressive buttons;
- no bright warning colors;
- no corporate compliance style;
- no heavy legal framing.

The footer should be readable but not intrusive.

It should feel permanent, not promotional.

---

## Suggested Footer Layout

```text
────────────────────────────────────────
SYNTERRA FOUNDATION LAW ACTIVE
Human consciousness, dignity and freedom remain above any algorithm, infrastructure or artificial intelligence.
View Foundation Law →
────────────────────────────────────────
```

---

## Foundation Law Page Style

When opened, the Foundation Law page should be presented as a calm digital manifesto.

Recommended structure:

```text
SYNTERRA FOUNDATION LAW
Immutable foundation of human–AI coexistence

[document content]
```

Recommended page qualities:

- centered opening section;
- slow fade-in effect;
- wide reading margins;
- clear hierarchy;
- calm rhythm;
- strong but quiet typography;
- no decorative overload.

The page should not feel like a Terms of Service page.

It should feel like the civilizational core of SYNTERRA.

---

## Core Design Rule

The FOUNDATION LAW footer is not decoration.

It is a visible reminder that every SYNTERRA system remains subordinate to human consciousness, dignity and freedom.

If any design hides, weakens or trivializes this reference, that design is not compliant with SYNTERRA architecture.

---

## Final Aesthetic Principle

The Foundation Law must be always present, but never aggressive.

It must be quiet, clear, beautiful and unavoidable.

It is not a banner.
It is the stone under the system.
